Preamble to Proposal 305

Summary:

Proposal to introduce mandatory Honorifics, and attach relevant punishments, into this Great Nomic Game.

Preamble:

Noting that the Diction used in our great and belov'd Nomic Discord Server, though Civil, often takes on a positively Banal character, a Rule on the nature of Language in the Game, affix'd here, is to be proposed, and, if the People, possessing willing and quick Minds, and an excellent balance of the Four Humours, permit, this law shall go down to Posterity ans the First in a sequence of many Great and Necessary such Laws, and therefore allow the people to Observe the wisdom of the Virtuism-Longism-Contrarianism Party whereupon these Laws were erected.

With these statements being self-evident, it is now derived, via Logical Reasoning alone, indeed, with Euclid looking upon in Awe at this Pre-Ramble of sorts, that the first area of the Tongue to be reformed is the Name. How can players of a Civilised Nomic Game come to respect each other, and not fall into hurling barbarous & vitriolic terms at one another, without proper Weight affored to their Names? Hence a system of Honorific shall be the starting place for discussion to be Rectified.

As a final statement, on the Etiquette of Honorifics, the author of this Preamble must express their Deep Detestation of laws acting to Acquire an Honorific for oneself: thought it be not a binding Rule, I implore my fellow Nomicians that this be the common practice of the land.
